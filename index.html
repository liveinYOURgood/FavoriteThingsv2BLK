IN NOV 2017, ON A LONG WEEKEND MINI VACATION VISITING MY BRO FROM MD: I took one of those Movie Tour Buses that started (9am) on Monday at the Santa Monica Pier and it drove me all over Los Angeles that day till 5pm. I went all over and saw many awesome things from a movie point of view. By the end of the day, I had a friendly wonderful connection to this great city where a few days ago I felt like a stranger, as it was my first time in California! But at 5:05pm, everything changed for the better! It was an amazing feeling! On this trip, I even dipped my hand into the Pacific Ocean and saw Muscle Beach. (at Venice Beach) I LOVE LA!

As a young guy (age 23), my brother and I got a part time job working as ushers for the Orlando Sports Complex back in 1993. The job lasted for like a year and a half.

What an opportunitity to earn extra money and see many exciting Concerts, NBA Basketball and American College Football Games, Kids Shows and More. A young Shaquille O'Neal was the star then and we all thought he would lead us soon to many championships for the home town Magic. (Opps...LOL) But in those days, it was just as exciting as seeing Guns N' Roses (Citrus Bowl) perform and Billy Joel and Bruce Springsteen (Orlando Arena) do their great thing. Also at the Citrus Bowl (today, it's called Camping World Stadium) one Saturday, I saw an African American Marching Band perform at half time of that ball game. It was exactly like that cool movie Drumline (2002) with Nick Cannon and Orlando Jones. Wow, so impressive!
